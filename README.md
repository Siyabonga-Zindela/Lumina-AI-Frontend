# Lumina

**Learn with an AI teacher that explains concepts in real time.**

Lumina is an AI-powered learning platform that teaches like a real tutor. Instead of prerecorded videos or simply providing answers, Lumina delivers interactive lessons, explains concepts step by step, answers questions naturally, and adapts to each learner's pace and understanding.

## Features

- Real-time AI teaching
- Personalized learning paths
- Adaptive explanations based on your understanding
- Interactive lessons with natural interruptions
- STEM-focused education

## Subjects

- Mathematics
- Physics
- Chemistry
- Computer Science
- Biology

## How It Works

1. Choose a topic.
2. Learn through a live AI lesson.
3. Ask questions at any time.
4. Build understanding as Lumina adapts to you.

## Vision

To make high-quality STEM education accessible through AI that teaches students how to think, not just how to find answers.
